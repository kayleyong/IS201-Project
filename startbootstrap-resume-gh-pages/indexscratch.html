Glasswing Butterflies

Home Learn More
  1. About
    • its scientific name is Greta oto
    • its Spanish name translates to "espejitos" which means "little mirrors"
    • this butterfly comes from the Nymphalidae family
  2. Diet
    • nectar of flowers
    • bird droppings
  3. Habitat
    • tropical rainforests
    • most commonly found from Central to South America (as far as Chile)
  4. Fun Facts
    • they can carry up to 40 times their own body weight
    • the tissue between their veins look like glass
    • their clear wings make it extremely difficult for predatory birds to track it in flight
